Hello, Taxi!

Relative taxi concentrations for a representative time

Outline

Finding a cab involves uncertainty. You may be looking at a peak hour or an impacted location. Hello, Taxi provides an estimate of available cab locations so you can reduce your wait by looking in the right places at the right time.

Models

Using data recently released by the city of New York, historical cab pickups and dropoffs are used to estimate sources and sinks of empty cabs on the grid. A simple transport model is used to propagate empty cabs and develop pickup wait times.

Trends

Other signals have emerged from the data. Ridership is insensitive to rain. Taxi traffic "sleeps in" an hour and a half on weekends. Business travelers return from the airport at higher rates Monday morning through Monday afternoon. Highest fare-per-hour is during early morning.
